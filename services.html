Our Services

Full-Service Design & Build Under One Roof

Skill sets honed over 7 years of experience in residential and commercial design — all meant to serve you better, from concept to completion.

01 — Design

Interior Design & Consultancy

Every great space begins with a clear vision. Our design team works closely with you to understand your lifestyle, brand identity, and functional requirements before translating them into cohesive design concepts.

  • Concept development & mood boards
  • Detailed 3D visualization & renders
  • Space planning & layout optimization
  • Material & finish selection guidance

02 — Build

Renovation & Construction

We handle the full scope of construction works with licensed trades and rigorous quality checks. Whether it's a condo refresh or a commercial fit-out from bare shell, our team delivers on spec and on schedule.

  • Structural works & hacking
  • Tiling, flooring & wall finishes
  • Plumbing & sanitary installations
  • Electrical wiring & lighting systems

03 — Craft

Custom Carpentry

Our in-house workshops fabricate bespoke furniture, cabinetry, and built-in solutions. This vertical integration gives us complete control over quality, dimensions, and delivery timelines.

  • Kitchen cabinets & wardrobes
  • Feature walls & TV consoles
  • Office workstations & reception counters
  • Retail display & salon stations

04 — Deliver

Project Management

Turnkey solutions from site supervision to final handover. We coordinate all trades, manage timelines, and keep you updated so you can focus on what matters while we handle the build.

  • Site supervision & progress reporting
  • Budget tracking & cost control
  • Contractor & supplier coordination
  • Defect inspection & final handover
